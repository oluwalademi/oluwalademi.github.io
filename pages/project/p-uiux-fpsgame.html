Context
This project envisions an immersive, engaging gaming platform designed to enhance player experience, streamline navigation, and optimize in-game interactions. Many gaming apps struggle with cluttered UI, poor onboarding experiences, and a lack of personalized content, leading to lower retention rates.
Challenge
The goal was to design a visually dynamic, intuitive gaming interface that improves game discovery, player engagement, and in-game purchases. This concept serves as a proof-of-design, showcasing an innovative approach to UX-UI for mobile gaming experiences.
Solution
Through competitive research and player feedback analysis, I identified poor menu navigation, lack of progress tracking, and unresponsive controls as key issues. My design introduces:
✅ A sleek, responsive UI optimized for touch controls.
✅ Seamless game discovery & personalized recommendations.
✅ Integrated social & multiplayer features to enhance engagement.
✅ Gamified UI elements for tracking progress, achievements, and rewards.
I developed a high-fidelity interactive prototype, ensuring smooth performance, engaging visuals, and an intuitive user flow.
Results
This concept serves as a showcase of my game UI/UX expertise, demonstrating my ability to create immersive, functional, and engaging interfaces for FPS games.
