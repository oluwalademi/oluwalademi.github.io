Food Delivery App
Role: UX-UI Designer
Responsibilities: User Research, Product Strategy, UX-UI Design, Prototyping
Type: Concept Project, B2C Mobile App
...
Context
This project envisions a seamless, user-friendly food delivery platform designed to simplify ordering, enhance restaurant discovery, and optimize the checkout process. Many existing food delivery apps struggle with cluttered interfaces, slow navigation, and confusing order tracking, leading to frustration and cart abandonment.
Challenge
The goal was to design an intuitive, visually appealing, and efficient food delivery interface that enhances user experience, improves order accuracy, and streamlines restaurant selection. This concept serves as a proof-of-design, demonstrating an innovative approach to UX-UI for food delivery services.
Solution
Through competitive analysis and user research, I identified checkout friction, lack of clear tracking, and inefficient restaurant filtering as major pain points. My design introduces:
✅ A clean, intuitive layout with easy restaurant browsing.
✅ Smart filtering & recommendations based on cuisine, location, and past orders.
✅ Seamless one-click checkout for faster transactions.
✅ Real-time order tracking with estimated delivery time and status updates.
I developed a high-fidelity interactive prototype, ensuring smooth navigation, clear CTAs, and an engaging user experience.
Results
This project showcases my ability to create efficient, user-centric interfaces that optimize the food ordering experience, demonstrating expertise in UX-UI for on-demand delivery services.
