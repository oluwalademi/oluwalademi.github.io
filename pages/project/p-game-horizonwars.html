Overview
Horizon Wars is an exhilarating shoot-'em-up game developed in the Unity engine, focusing on fast-paced space combat and responsive controls.
Players pilot a dynamic spaceship through the vast expanse of space, engaging in intense battles against relentless waves of enemies.
Designed to deliver an adrenaline-fueled experience, the game emphasizes fluid movement, precise shooting mechanics, and visually striking effects.
Technical Design
"Horizon Wars" is a high-intensity shoot-'em-up game developed in Unity, focusing on responsive spaceship controls, dynamic enemy behaviors, and engaging arcade-style gameplay. Players navigate a customizable starfighter through a richly layered 2.5D space environment, fending off relentless waves of enemies while avoiding environmental hazards. A robust enemy spawning system controls wave logic, pacing, and randomized attack patterns for replayability. The player ship support a modular weapon system, managed via a lightweight component-based structure.
